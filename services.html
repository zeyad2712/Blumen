Services

Services at the Blumen florist shop:

Online Store: the ability to order flowers and plants online with delivery to your home or office.

Consultations on plant care: store specialists will help you choose suitable plants and tell you about the features of caring for them.

Florist services: creating bouquets and compositions from fresh flowers, as well as interior design using living plants.

Training and master classes: conducting seminars and master classes on floristry and plant care.
